What We Do

Our club is made up of a team of dedicated students who work together to give our fellow speech and debators the help that they deserve!

View our events

Weekly Meetings

Every week, there will be meetings at which students can ask questions, prepare for tournaments, and learn about their event.

Writing and Editing

A major part of speech and debate is writing cases and editing speeches. With our team of experienced captains, we make it easy and fun for students to learn.

Live Practice

Practice makes perfect and here at AHS S&D, we take that very seriously. During the meetings, students will have the time to practice in front of a live audience and receive feedback to become better speakers.

Tournaments

Our team goes to at least one tournament every month, and these competitions are a great way for students to showcase the amazing skills that they have learned.
